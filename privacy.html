Trover AI

Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2025

The short version: Trover AI stores your browsing data locally on your device only. We do not collect, transmit, or sell your personal information. Your data never leaves your browser.

What data does Trover AI access?

Trover AI reads the text content of web pages you visit in order to build a local, searchable memory. This includes:

  • Page titles and URLs
  • Visible text content of pages (product info, articles, search results)
  • The domain and page type (article, product, video, etc.)
  • Timestamps of when you visited a page

Where is my data stored?

All data is stored exclusively in your browser's local extension storage (chrome.storage.local) using AES-256-GCM encryption. This storage is:

  • Local to your device — never uploaded to any server
  • Encrypted at rest with a key generated on your device
  • Deleted when you uninstall the extension or use the "Clear History" option

What pages are excluded?

Trover AI automatically skips sensitive pages and will never store content from:

  • Email services (Gmail, Outlook, Yahoo Mail, ProtonMail)
  • Banking, payment, and checkout pages
  • Login, password, and authentication pages
  • Medical, legal, and therapy-related pages
  • Private messaging platforms (WhatsApp Web, Telegram, Twitter DMs)
  • Any page whose URL or title contains sensitive keywords

Does Trover AI send my data anywhere?

When you ask a question in the chat panel, your browsing context and question are sent to an AI provider (DeepSeek or OpenAI) via a secure HTTPS proxy in order to generate an answer. Specifically:

  • Only the text relevant to your question is included in the API request
  • Requests are routed through a Cloudflare Worker proxy — no data is logged or stored by this proxy
  • The AI provider receives only the content needed to answer your question; it does not receive your identity or account information

Outside of answering questions, no data is ever sent anywhere.

API keys

If you provide an API key, it is encrypted using AES-256-GCM and stored only in your browser's local storage. It is never transmitted to anyone other than the AI provider (DeepSeek or OpenAI) when you ask a question.

Do you collect analytics or usage data?

No. Trover AI does not include any analytics, crash reporting, or telemetry. We have no visibility into how you use the extension.

Do you sell my data?

No. We do not sell, share, or monetize your data in any way.

Children's privacy

Trover AI is not directed at children under the age of 13 and we do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to this policy

If we make material changes to this policy, we will update the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the extension after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
